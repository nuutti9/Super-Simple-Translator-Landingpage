Privacy Policy for Super Simple Translator

Effective date: 13th of April 2026

Super Simple Translator ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application, Super Simple Translator.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

Account Information

- Email address
- Name or profile information provided by Apple Sign In or Google Sign-In
- User account ID

App Preferences

- Selected app language
- Preferred translation languages
- Voice settings
- Other in-app preferences

Subscription Information

- Subscription status
- Purchase and entitlement information provided by Apple and RevenueCat

Audio and Translation Data

- Audio you record for speech translation
- Text generated from speech recognition
- Translated text
- App interaction data necessary to provide the translation service

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and operate the app
- Authenticate your account
- Save your preferences across devices
- Process and restore subscriptions
- Improve app functionality and reliability
- Provide support and respond to requests
- Meet legal obligations

3. Speech and Translation Processing

Super Simple Translator processes audio and text you provide in order to deliver translation features. Audio and text may be transmitted to third-party service providers only as necessary to perform speech recognition, translation, text-to-speech, authentication, database storage, and subscription management.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party providers such as:

- Supabase for authentication and user data storage
- RevenueCat for subscription management
- Apple and Google for sign-in and platform services
- AI, speech recognition, and text-to-speech providers required to deliver app functionality

These third parties may process your information only as needed to provide their services.

5. Data Retention

We retain account, preference, and subscription-related information for as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data where required by law, except where retention is legally required.

6. Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your account from within the app. When your account is deleted, associated account data stored by us will be deleted, subject to legal and operational requirements.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share your information only:

- With service providers that help us operate the app
- When required by law
- To protect our legal rights, safety, and security

8. Children's Privacy

Super Simple Translator is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

10. International Users

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, where our service providers operate.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

- Access your personal data
- Correct your personal data
- Delete your personal data
- Object to or restrict certain processing

To make a request, contact us at support@nodalapps.com.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above.
